Before you can start, it is mandatory that you need to have a business domain email address to get going. Once you have it, you will have to click on the Job Icon (Briefcase Icon) on the header or in the post dialog box. A Job form will open now.
Fill the form shown to you with necessary fields.
Click on "Save". That's all. Your job will be shown to people in your connections.
FYI, if you need your job to be shown to people beyond your connections, you may consider upgrading your job which will list it under featured ones whereby it increases the odds of getting it applied sooner! Sumo!
Before you can start, it is mandatory that you need to have a business domain email address to get going, unless you are either a student or a job seeker. Once you have it, you will have to click on the Candidate Icon on the header or in the post dialog box. A Candidate form will open now.
Fill the form shown to you with necessary fields.
Click on "Save". That's all. Your candidate will be shown to people in your connections.
FYI, if you need your candidate to be shown to people beyond your connections, you may consider upgrading your job which will list it under featured ones whereby it increases the odds of getting it applied sooner! Sumo!
